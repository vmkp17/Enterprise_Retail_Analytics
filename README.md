# Enterprise_Retail_Analytics
End-to-End Retail Analytics Project using Python, SQL, and Power BI with interactive dashboards and business insights.


# 🛍️ Enterprise Retail Intelligence & Customer Analytics Platform

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Enterprise Retail Intelligence & Customer Analytics Platform** is an end-to-end data analytics project that transforms raw retail transaction data into actionable business insights using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.

The project demonstrates the complete data analytics lifecycle—from business understanding and exploratory data analysis (EDA) to SQL querying and interactive dashboard development.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate large volumes of customer transaction data every day. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to understand customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.

This project helps business stakeholders answer key questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers contribute the most revenue?
- Which payment methods are preferred?
- How does customer purchasing behavior vary?
- What business strategies can increase revenue?

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Measure overall business performance.
- Identify high-performing products and categories.
- Understand customer demographics.
- Analyze payment methods and purchase frequency.
- Build interactive dashboards for decision-making.
